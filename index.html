I’m a curious INTP-passionate about learning, understanding and improving systems. By day, I’m a programme manager driving organisational transformation; by night, I’m a maker, diving into coding, DIY electronics, 3d printing, homebrewing, or whatever new interest has taken hold.

My journey into full-stack development began with a fascination for self-hosting, initially driven by a desire to regain digital soverignty. That process ignited an interest fundamentals of backend architecture, service deployment, and digital infrastructure. Pairing this with frontend technologies, I’m now focused on building seamless, user-friendly and progressive web applications.

I love to craft lean solutions that reflect my commitment to sustainability and low-impact. Whether it’s developing tools for task automation, designing modular web apps, or creating systems that empower others to be their best selves, I aim to make technology work smarter and more sustainably, for the benefit of all.
