What is RideFixr?

RideFixr is the ultimate companion app for bicycle maintenance and repair. Whether you're a casual rider or enthusiast, we help you stay on top of bike care with timely service reminders, step-by-step repair guides, and gamification to keep you motivated. Plus, you'll benefit from community-driven insights to master bike maintenance like a pro. We’re confident you’ll love RideFixr—that’s why you can try it for free. In the long run, it’ll save you money and keep your bike in top condition. Best of all, it works seamlessly on any device.

Key Features

RideFixr empowers cyclists to proactively maintain their bikes, saving money and maximizing riding enjoyment through personalized guidance, smart diagnostics, and convenient resources:

Step-by-Step Repair Guides: Easy-to-follow tutorials for fixing common bike issues.
Level up: Earn XP and track progress to stay motivated.
Community Insights: Connect with fellow cyclists for tips, tricks, and advice.
Personalised Recommendations: Maintenance schedules tailored to your bike typical usage.
Offline Mode: Download your data, and use it anywhere, even on the road.
